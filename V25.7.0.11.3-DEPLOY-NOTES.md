# v25.7.0.11.3 — Font readability bump across animation engines

Small hotfix. Fonts in both animation engines (ProcessAnimation +
TimelineThresholdAnimation) were too small for comfortable reading.
This release bumps every font under 12px by +2 points — leaving fonts
already at 12px or above untouched.

## Pedagogical insight (OBS-027)

**Compaction tradeoffs need to be defended on legibility, not vertical
fit alone.** v25.7.0.11.2 traded font size for vertical-fit savings —
that was the wrong tradeoff for a training app where Caribbean bank
staff use varying monitors at varying distances. Vertical scrolling
within a section is an acceptable cost; unreadable text is not.

## What changed and why

Every fontSize value below 12px in ProcessAnimation.jsx and
TimelineThresholdAnimation.jsx was bumped by +2 points. Examples:

- Stat values: 10.5 → 12.5
- Stat labels: 9 → 11
- Panel name: 14 → still 14 (no bump above 12)
- Panel descriptor: 11.5 → 13.5
- Account meta: 10 → 12
- Chart axis labels (J$ scale): 9 → 11
- Threshold line label: 9.5 → 11.5
- Week markers: 8.5 → 10.5
- Y-axis title (DEPOSIT AMOUNT): 9 → 11
- Caption strip text: stayed at 12 (already readable)
- Stage indicator: stayed at 11 (uppercase tracked, conventionally smaller)
- Control label: stayed where it was
- Control meta: 11 → 13
- Control callout ("Would have flagged X of Y"): 11.5 → 13.5
- Control stage hint ("Active at stage X..."): 11 → 13
- Naive badge: 8.5 → 10.5

Total: 31 fonts bumped in ProcessAnimation, 28 fonts bumped in
TimelineThresholdAnimation. 59 changes total. Mechanical bumps; no
restructuring.

## Honest accounting

1. **This release does NOT change the control-stage hint logic** from
   v25.7.0.11.2. The hint should still appear underneath the "Would
   have flagged X of Y" callout on cards where the toggled control
   isn't surfacing a signal at the current stage. If after this
   readability bump you still don't see the italic hint text on
   cards like "Reduce employee LinkedIn footprint" at stage 7 of
   F1067, that's a separate logic bug to debug — not a font bump.
   Flag it after this deploy.

2. **Some fonts I deliberately did NOT bump:** values already at 12px
   or above (caption strip body text, button labels, headings) and
   uppercase mono-spaced UI labels at 11px (these are tracked
   typography conventions where smaller-with-letter-spacing reads as
   "label" rather than "content"). If after deploy any of those
   still feel too small, tell me which ones and I'll bump them too.

3. **Mechanical +2 across the board** is a deliberate choice. I
   considered case-by-case bumps but those tend to introduce
   inconsistency. A uniform bump preserves the typographic hierarchy
   (whatever was relatively smaller stays relatively smaller) just
   shifted up by a constant.
