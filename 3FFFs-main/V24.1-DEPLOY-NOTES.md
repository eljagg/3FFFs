# v24.1 Deployment Notes — dark-mode contrast + answered-state highlights

**What this fixes:**
- Body narrative, signals, and option text were too pale in dark mode.
- The v24 disabled-button pattern made option text unreadable before
  the user picked a confidence level.
- Answered options didn't stand out — no clear "your pick" or "right answer"
  signal post-decision.

**Scope:** wider sweep. The fix lives in `client/src/index.css` so it
benefits every page (Home, Coverage, Quiz, Tutor, Framework, etc.) — not
just the Scenario page where the bug was reported.

## What changed, in detail

### 1. CSS tokens (the foundation fix)

Dark mode `--ink-soft` and `--ink-faint` were borderline against the dark
paper. Lifted to comfortable WCAG ratios:

| Token | v24 value | v24.1 value | Contrast against `--paper-hi` (#1e1d19) |
|-------|-----------|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| `--ink`        | `#e8e4d8` | `#efece1` | 13 → 14.5 : 1 |
| `--ink-soft`   | `#c4bfb0` | `#d8d3c1` | 8.5 → 11.5 : 1 |
| `--ink-faint`  | `#908a7b` | `#a9a292` | 4.4 → 6.0 : 1 |

Accent and status tokens (`--accent`, `--success`, `--warning`, `--danger`)
also lightened so they read as foreground on the dark paper, not as muted
decoration. Light mode tokens are unchanged — the existing values were fine
on the cream paper.

### 2. Scenario.jsx — text now uses higher tier of contrast tokens

Where v24 used `--ink-soft` for body text and `--ink-faint` for labels, v24.1
uses `--ink` and `--ink-soft` respectively. One step up the contrast ladder
across:

- The scenario summary (top of the page)
- The stage narrative ("Cash pickups from WU are structured into deposits…")
- The signal text
- The "Stage X of Y" label, "Your decision" label, "Signals observed" label
- The technique chip ("F1067 · Gather Victim Information")
- The completion panel summary text and the calibration recap

### 3. The disabled-options fix (the actual bug from your screenshot)

**Before:** options were rendered at `opacity: 0.6` until confidence was
picked. Dark mode + 60% opacity made the text nearly unreadable.

**After:**
- Options stay at full opacity always.
- The confidence slider itself pulses gently when no choice is picked.
- A small accent-colored line above the options says "Pick a confidence
  level above before choosing an option" until confidence is set, then fades.
- Buttons remain non-clickable (cursor stays default) until confidence is set.

### 4. Answered-state highlights

Once a question is answered:
- **Picked option:** thicker border (2px), saturated background (red or green),
  font-weight 500, plus a "YOUR PICK" pill in the top-right (red if wrong,
  green if right).
- **Correct option** (when not picked): green border, "RIGHT ANSWER" pill.
- **Other wrong options:** opacity 0.55 — visible enough to read the rationale
  on hover, dim enough not to compete for attention.
- **Branching consequence wrong picks** still show the "Branches →" hint.

The existing "shake on wrong answer" micro-interaction is preserved.

### 5. Floating Tutor button no longer overlaps content

`StagePanel` gained `paddingBottom: 72` so the bottom-right "Ask the Tutor"
button has clearance and never sits on top of the rationale text.
