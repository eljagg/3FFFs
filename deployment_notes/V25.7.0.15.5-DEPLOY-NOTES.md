# v25.7.0.15.5 — Audio muted by default; ElevenLabs pivot deferred

Decision-driven release, not bug-driven. After 5 iterations of the
audio narration system (v25.7.0.15 → v25.7.0.15.4), Omar's testing
across Chrome incognito and Microsoft Edge demonstrated that browser
TTS quality is **insufficient for the realism objective**, regardless
of architectural fixes. We've hit the quality ceiling of bundled OS
voices (Microsoft David, Google US English, etc.).

This release ships the right interim state: audio MUTED BY DEFAULT,
opt-in via the toggle, with documentation for the eventual ElevenLabs
pivot when the subscription becomes feasible.

## Pedagogical insight (OBS-027)

**Bad audio is worse than no audio.** Five iterations of architectural
fixes (sequential queue, Chrome keep-alive, race fix, calibrated
scripts) addressed real bugs but didn't solve the underlying problem:
trainees were hearing system TTS reading text, not realistic fraudster
delivery. A trainee whose first impression of the Vishing animation
is "this audio sounds robotic" learns to discount the audio
component — even if a future release improves it. Better to ship
silent visuals (which work) and let the trainee opt in to audio if
they're curious, than to push robotic audio as the default experience.

## Honest accounting

Three notes:

1. **This is the right call.** Five releases of audio iteration is
   too many. Cutting losses, shipping the working visual experience
   silently, and locking the audio quality fix to a real solution
   (ElevenLabs) is the correct response to repeated symptom
   recurrence.

2. **The audio architecture wasn't wasted.** Every fix from
   v25.7.0.15.1 through v25.7.0.15.4 will still apply when we pivot
   to ElevenLabs (sequential queue → matters for chained MP3s,
   keep-alive → harmless and stays, race fix → still relevant).
   The 38 authored audio lines are the script for ElevenLabs
   generation. Nothing thrown away.

3. **Picking up the ElevenLabs pivot is a clean Phase task.** When
   subscription is feasible, ELEVENLABS-PIVOT.md is the spec —
   3-4 hours of focused work and audio quality is solved permanently.
   Don't try to half-pivot or partially regenerate; commit to the
   full plan when the time is right.
